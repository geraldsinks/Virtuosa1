Seller Guide

Best practices and compliance guidelines for successful selling on Virtuosa

Quick Start Checklist

1. Complete Your Profile

Verify your student status, add profile photo, and complete all required information.

2. Read This Guide

Familiarize yourself with our best practices, policies, and compliance requirements.

3. Prepare Your Items

Clean your items, take quality photos, and gather all necessary information.

4. Create Your First Listing

Write detailed descriptions, set competitive prices, and publish your listing.

5. Manage Sales

Respond to inquiries, process orders promptly, and maintain good communication.

Creating Quality Listings

Image Quality Standards

Required
  • Use natural, bright lighting - no dark or blurry photos
  • Capture all angles - front, back, sides, and details
  • Show scale - include common objects for size reference
  • Highlight flaws - photograph any damage or wear honestly
  • Use plain background - avoid clutter or distracting elements
  • Minimum 3 photos per item, maximum 10 photos
  • Images must be at least 800x800 pixels
  • No watermarks, text, or promotional overlays

Writing Effective Descriptions

Best Practice
  • Be specific - include brand, model, size, color, and condition
  • Describe condition honestly - use standard grading (New, Like New, Good, Fair)
  • Include measurements - provide exact dimensions and weight
  • Mention included accessories - list all items buyer will receive
  • Explain unique features - highlight what makes your item special
  • Use proper formatting - bullet points and paragraphs for readability
  • Avoid promotional language - focus on facts, not marketing hype
  • Include care instructions if applicable

Pricing Best Practices

Research Market

  • • Check similar items on Virtuosa
  • • Research prices on other platforms
  • • Consider original retail price
  • • Factor in current market demand

Competitive Pricing

  • • Price below retail for used items
  • • Consider Virtuosa's commission fees
  • • Leave room for negotiation
  • • Offer bundle discounts for multiple items

Value Proposition

  • • Highlight unique selling points
  • • Emphasize condition and quality
  • • Mention any included extras
  • • Consider campus convenience factor

Code of Conduct

Required Behaviors

  • Respond to buyer inquiries within 24 hours
  • Be honest and accurate in all descriptions
  • Maintain professional and respectful communication
  • Honor agreed-upon prices and terms
  • Ship or deliver items within promised timeframe
  • Provide tracking information when applicable
  • Resolve issues professionally and promptly
  • Maintain positive seller ratings above 4.0 stars

Prohibited Behaviors

  • Misrepresenting item condition or authenticity
  • Using inappropriate language or harassment
  • Pressure selling or aggressive tactics
  • Canceling orders without valid reason
  • Sharing personal contact information
  • Engaging in transactions outside Virtuosa
  • Creating fake reviews or ratings
  • Discriminating against any buyers

Compliance & Legal Requirements

Prohibited Items

  • • Weapons, firearms, ammunition
  • • Alcohol, tobacco, vaping products
  • • Controlled substances or drug paraphernalia
  • • Stolen or counterfeit goods
  • • Adult content or sexual services
  • • Hate speech or discriminatory materials
  • • Hazardous materials or chemicals
  • • Live animals or pets

Legal Obligations

  • • You must be the legal owner of items sold
  • • Ensure items comply with campus policies
  • • Follow local, state, and federal laws
  • • Pay applicable taxes on earnings
  • • Respect intellectual property rights
  • • Maintain proper business licenses if required
  • • Comply with consumer protection laws
  • • Keep records of all transactions

Communication Standards

Best Practices

  • Use Virtuosa's messaging system for all communication
  • Be prompt - respond within 24 hours
  • Be clear and concise in your responses
  • Provide additional photos or information when requested
  • Set clear expectations for pickup/delivery
  • Be flexible and reasonable with buyers
  • Document important agreements in writing
  • Maintain professional tone at all times

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Sharing personal phone numbers or social media
  • Using slang, abbreviations, or unclear language
  • Making promises you can't keep
  • Ignoring buyer questions or concerns
  • Being defensive when receiving feedback
  • Rushing buyers to make decisions
  • Changing terms after agreement is made
  • Disappearing after payment is received
