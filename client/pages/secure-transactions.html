How We Secure Your Transactions

1. User Verification

Both buyers and sellers must complete identity verification before participating in transactions.

2. Secure Listing

Product listings are reviewed for authenticity and compliance with our guidelines.

3. Protected Payment

Payments are processed securely and held in escrow until both parties confirm transaction completion.

4. Safe Exchange

We facilitate safe pickup/delivery options and provide guidelines for secure in-person meetings.

5. Confirmation & Release

Once both parties confirm successful exchange, funds are released to the seller.

6. Ongoing Support

24/7 support available for any issues or disputes that may arise.

Security Best Practices

For Buyers

  • Always verify the seller's profile and ratings before purchasing
  • Use our secure messaging system for all communications
  • Never share personal financial information directly with sellers
  • Meet in public places for in-person exchanges
  • Inspect items carefully before confirming receipt
  • Keep records of all transactions and communications
  • Report suspicious activity immediately

For Sellers

  • Provide accurate descriptions and clear photos of items
  • Respond promptly to buyer questions and messages
  • Use our platform for all payment processing
  • Meet in safe, public locations for exchanges
  • Keep proof of shipment for delivered items
  • Maintain good communication throughout the process
  • Build your reputation through positive reviews

Verified Users Only

All users must verify their student status and identity before buying or selling on our platform.

Important Security Warnings

  • • Never share your password or financial details with anyone
  • • Avoid transactions outside our secure platform
  • • Be cautious of deals that seem too good to be true
  • • Report users who ask for payment via wire transfer or gift cards
  • • Never meet buyers/sellers in private or isolated locations

Pro Tips for Secure Trading

  • • Enable two-factor authentication on your account
  • • Use strong, unique passwords for your account
  • • Regularly review your transaction history
  • • Keep your app and browser updated
  • • Trust your instincts - if something feels wrong, report it
