Frequently Asked Questions

Which payment method is best for me?

Mobile Money is recommended for users in Zambia due to low fees and instant processing. Credit cards offer the best buyer protection, while cash on delivery is ideal for local pickups.

Are my payment details secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and never store your complete payment details on our servers.

Can I get a refund if something goes wrong?

Yes, most payment methods offer buyer protection and chargeback options. See our Refund Policy for details.

How long do payments take to process?

Credit cards and mobile money process instantly. Cash on delivery processes when you receive and pay for your item.

What mobile money services are supported?

We support MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, and Zamtel Money for all transactions within Zambia.
